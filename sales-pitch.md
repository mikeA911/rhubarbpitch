Q: What if I can't commit 15-20 hours/month? A: Consider Tier 2 (10-15 hours) or Tier 3 (5-10 hours). We want quality over quantity.
Q: Can I lose my equity? A: Yes, if you don't fulfill responsibilities or leave before 1-year cliff. After that, you keep vested equity even if you leave.
Q: What if Rhubarb fails? A: Equity becomes worthless, but you've still earned advisory fees and project income along the way. Typical startup risk.
Q: Can I sell my equity? A: Not until an exit event (acquisition or IPO) or unless the company approves. You can't sell to external parties without approval.
Q: Do I have to curate content forever? A: Active curation required during vesting period (4 years). After that, you can reduce involvement but retain equity.
Q: What's the tax treatment? A: Equity is typically taxed as capital gains when sold (not income). Consult your tax advisor. We use standard ISOs (Incentive Stock Options).
